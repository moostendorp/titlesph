How Our Title Transfer Service Works

Step 1: Document Preparation

We help you collect all necessary documents, such as the Deed of Sale, Tax Declaration, and government-required forms.

Step 2: Filing and Submission

We ensure all required documents are filed with the appropriate government agencies, such as the Registry of Deeds and BIR, for a smooth transaction.

Step 3: Monitoring the Process

Our team tracks the progress of your title transfer and ensures the process stays on track.

Step 4: Completion

We make sure the title is successfully transferred and provide you with the new Certificate of Title.

Why Choose Our Title Transfer Service?

  • Timely and Reliable: We ensure your title transfer is completed quickly and smoothly.
  • Track Your Progress: Stay updated with real-time status via your personalized Trello board.

What You’ll Need for a Title Transfer

  • Deed of Sale (or other proof of ownership)
  • Tax Declaration and latest tax payment receipts
  • Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) or Condominium Certificate of Title (CCT)
  • Government-issued IDs
  • Additional requirements if inherited or subdivided
